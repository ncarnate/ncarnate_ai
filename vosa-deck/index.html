Introducing FAT Agents

Unlike single-task oriented thin agents, FAT agents can learn a broad spectrum of workflows. FAT agents, like people, have a unique coherent identity that spans many software systems and conversations.

FOUNDATIONAL IDENTITY

Agents operate inside the systems you already use – email, Slack, ERP, CRM – interacting through familiar platforms without extra logins or software. They carry a true enterprise identity, like a trusted teammate.

ADAPTIVE CONTEXT

Agents' context is dynamically shaped to the task at hand – surfacing only the relevant data and tools, filtering noise, and enforcing security so they see only what they should.

TRAINABLE (HUMAN-IN-THE-LOOP)

Workflows are defined and improved in plain English. Owners refine instructions through natural feedback, with agents learning from both people and their own observations to continuously improve.
